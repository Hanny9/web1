Rainy Season 07/01

Refer to this link

The rainy season comes around every summer in Korea. We get a lot of rain during the season. It usually begins in mid-June and lasts until July. When it rains, it often rains cats and dogs.

The annual precipitation in Korea is concentrated in summer because of the rainy season. People always carry an umbrella around during the wet season. After the rainy season, the sweltering heat of summer begins in earnest.
